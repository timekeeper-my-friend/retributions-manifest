Researchers are designated by medieval nobility titles. They are listed here, from highest to lowest in terms of power and importance.
	King
	Prince
	Marquis
	Count
	Viscount
	Baron
	Lord
	Knight
These titles are not gendered. Researchers are also further divided into Greek letters, signifying their division. Finally, specific researchers are given a number and letter that corresponds to this. Look at [[Research Statement 3042]] for a specific way on how to write researcher names.
